Jian Xie

I am Jian Xie (谢健), a first-year master at Fudan University in School of Computer Science, Shanghai, China. I am advised by Prof. Yanghua Xiao at Knowledge Works Lab and Prof. Yu Su at the The Ohio State University.

I have a broad interest in Natural Language Processing, with a current focus on Large Language Models and their potential hazards. I am fascinated by the capabilities and challenges associated with these models, particularly in areas such as understanding, generation, and ethical considerations.
