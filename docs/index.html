Jian Xie

I am Jian Xie (谢健), a second-year master at Fudan University in School of Computer Science, Shanghai, China. I am advised by Prof. Yanghua Xiao at Knowledge Works Lab. Previously, I interned at Ohio State University and was advised by Prof. Yu Su, working on language agents.

I have a broad interest in Natural Language Processing, with a current focus on Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) and Language Agents. My recent work dives into the knowledge preferences of LLMs in RAG scenarios and the planning/grounding ability of language agents. Feel free to contact me if you are interested in my work or want to discuss a potential collaboration!

I'm looking for a U.S. PhD position in 2025 Fall. I am interested in RAG, language agents, and world models. Don't hesitate to email me if there is a potential opportunity.
